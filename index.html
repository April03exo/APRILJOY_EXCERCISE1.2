About Me

I'm April Joy V. Rapanut. I may be 20 but still act like a child sometimes. You can find me in Nagtupacan, Sta. Maria Ilocos Sur or at our church, which has always been my second home.


I dream of traveling the world, experiencing different cultures, capturing breathtaking sunsets, and writing my journey in my diary. I also hope to build a career in technology where I can create something useful, but at the same time live a life filled with faith and creativity.

Skills

My skills include sketching, which helps me relax and allows my imagination to run free, as well as photography, where I enjoy capturing the beauty of nature and the little details that often go unnoticed. Through sketching, I can express my emotions on paper, while photography gives me the chance to preserve memories.

Hobbies
